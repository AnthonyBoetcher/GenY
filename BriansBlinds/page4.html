About Us

Brians Blinds was founded in 2022 by a Capetownian who is SERIOUSLY passionate about blinds. The only thing Brian is more passionate about, is SUNBLOCK! (see his other startup, BriansSkinShade) Brian, being the keen engineer that he is, has grown increasingly concerned with the lack of quality blind suppliers and blind engineers in Cape Town, and so he has taken it upon himself to singlehandedly address this very alarming deficit. Brian studied Engineering, and consequently decided to complete his Masters and Doctorate in Blind Engineering and Mechanics, with a special interest in the action of sunlight on... Everything!

Now we might not all be as passionate about blinds as Brian. But you know that you are in safe hands.
